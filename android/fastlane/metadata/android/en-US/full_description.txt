Simple, but feature-packed. Cove is a Bitcoin wallet that grows with you—from beginner to advanced coin management—while keeping your private keys under your control.

HOT WALLET
• Create a wallet in seconds
• Private keys encrypted locally on your device
• Biometric and PIN security

HARDWARE WALLET SUPPORT
• Coldcard, TAPSIGNER, Krux, Jade, SeedSigner, and Foundation Passport
• PSBT signing via QR (BBQr & UR), NFC, and file import
• SeedQR backup and restore
• Xpub and descriptor export as QR
• Watch-only wallet support

FULL COIN CONTROL
• Select, label, and track your UTXOs
• Advanced search, filtering, and sorting
• BIP329 label sync with desktop wallets
• CSV export with historical fiat prices for tax reporting

SECURITY FIRST
• Decoy & Wipe PINs for emergency situations
• BIP39 recovery phrase verification
• 100% on-device operations
• No data ever leaves your phone

YOUR WALLET, YOUR WAY
• Native Segwit, Wrapped Segwit, and Legacy addresses
• Beautiful light & dark modes
• Custom fiat currency selection
• Connect to your own Electrum or Esplora node
• Custom fee selection
• Transaction notes with BIP329 labels

OPEN SOURCE
Cove is 100% open source and auditable. Verify the code yourself at github.com/bitcoinppl/cove
